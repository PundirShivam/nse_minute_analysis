- Study concludes (cites) best hedge would be long volatility 
- VIX Index is not tradable and VIX derivatives are expensive
- VIX Index (Volatility) can be replicated by holding combination of SPX options
- The options which can be characterized to be the most responsible for driving the movements of the VIX index are ITM puts and ATM calls
- The best performance can be made by buying the ITM-OTM put spread and selling the ATM-OTM call spread. 
- options provides the portfolio with access to long volatility exposition while maintaining cost-efficiency
- It is set to be the point in time when the value of the VIX index is lower than the mean of VIX plus one standard deviation
